PanoEnabler (PanoMod)
===========

Enable Panorama and Customize it.

Supported Devices
===========

- iPhone 4 (1MP Panorama sensor only)
- iPhone 4s
- iPhone 5
- iPhone 5c
- iPhone 5s
- iPod touch 4G
- iPod touch 5G (32/64 GB)
- iPad 2
- iPad 3
- iPad 4
- iPad Air
- iPad Air 2
- iPad mini 1G
- iPad mini 2G

Tested in iOS 6.0 - 8.1
